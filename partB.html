Notes

This is a memory-based matching game. The player must match pairs of pictures in a grid. Before starting, the player chooses the difficulty level and the number of picture pairs. The game briefly shows all pictures face-up to memorize their locations, then flips them face-down. The player must click on pairs of hidden pictures to reveal and match them. The game is timed, and the goal is to match all pairs before the timer runs out. The memorization time varies based on the chosen difficulty level: 3 seconds for hard, 5 seconds for medium, and 8 seconds for easy. The game is timed, with a countdown timer ticking down during play. The timer is set to 120 seconds for 8 pairs of pictures, 150 seconds for 10 pairs, and 180 seconds for 12 pairs.
